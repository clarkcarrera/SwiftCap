About SwiftCap

New York City, NY

SwiftCap is a direct lender providing fast, flexible working capital to small and mid-sized businesses across the United States. We specialize in Merchant Cash Advances (MCA) ranging from $100,000 up to $2,000,000, designed to help businesses scale without the friction of traditional bank financing.

Why Work With a Direct Lender?

Unlike brokerages that shop your application around, we are the direct source of your funding. This means:

  • Faster Decisions
  • Better Terms
  • Clear Communication
  • Consistent Service
  • Long Term Partnerships

Our approach is simple: Speed. Transparency. Alignment.

We evaluate businesses based on real performance, not perfect credit, and deploy capital quickly so owners can act on opportunities when timing matters most.
